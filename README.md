# Artificial Intelligence for Communication and Marketing

This repository includes three data science and machine learning projects developed during the university course *Artificial Intelligence for Communication and Marketing*.  
Each project focuses on applying AI techniques to real-world business and marketing problems.

---

## 1. Customer Churn Prediction
**Objective:** Predict which telecom customers are likely to leave the service.  
**Techniques:** Extreme Gradient Boosting (XGBoost), CatBoost, feature engineering, and cross-validation.  
**Skills demonstrated:**  
- Data preprocessing and cleaning  
- Model training and evaluation  
- Business-oriented interpretation of classification results  

---

## 2. Marketing Mix Modeling
**Objective:** Estimate the effect of online and offline advertising channels on e-commerce sales.  
**Techniques:** Linear regression, correlation analysis, and ROI computation.  
**Skills demonstrated:**  
- Econometric modeling and channel efficiency analysis  
- Adstock-like adjustments and baseline sales simulation  
- Visualization of marketing performance metrics  

---

## 3. Cluster Analysis
**Objective:** Segment bank customers into groups based on demographic and behavioral variables.  
**Techniques:** K-Means clustering, correlation analysis, and exploratory data visualization.  
**Skills demonstrated:**  
- Unsupervised learning and feature transformation  
- Interpretation of customer segments  
- Data-driven strategy formulation
